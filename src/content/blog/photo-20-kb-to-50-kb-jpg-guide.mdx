## Step-by-step workflow

1. Read the form requirement before editing.
2. Crop to the required ratio first.
3. Resize dimensions before reducing quality.
4. Export close to the upper allowed limit.
5. Open the final JPG and confirm face or text detail is still readable.

## FAQ

### Should I choose 20 KB or 50 KB?

Use the largest file size the form allows unless it gives a strict exact value.

### Can I compress a phone photo into this range?

Yes, but you will usually need to resize dimensions and reduce quality.

### Does changing JPG quality change dimensions?

No. Quality changes compression level. Dimensions change width and height.
