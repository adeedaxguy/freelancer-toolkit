## FAQ

### How do I calculate 20 percent Fiverr fees?

Multiply the order amount by 0.20. Subtract that amount from the order to estimate seller net before taxes and withdrawal costs.

### How do I price a gig to net $100?

At a 20 percent fee, divide $100 by 0.80. The gross gig price would be $125 before tax and payout considerations.

### Is 20 percent always exact?

Use it as a planning model and confirm the actual fee Fiverr displays for your account and order.
