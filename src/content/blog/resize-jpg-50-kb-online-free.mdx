## FAQ

### Can I resize JPG to 50 KB without uploading to a server?

FreelTools image tools process images in the browser, so the workflow is designed to avoid server upload for the edit.

### What size in pixels is best for 50 KB?

It depends on image detail. Start around 600 by 600 for profile-style photos, then adjust quality.

### Will 50 KB always look good?

No. Very detailed images may show artifacts at 50 KB. Crop and resize before lowering quality too far.
