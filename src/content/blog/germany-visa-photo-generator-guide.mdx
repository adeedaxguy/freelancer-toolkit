## What causes rejections

Most rejected photos fail for practical reasons:

- The crop is too close or too far away.
- The background has shadows, texture, or objects.
- The face is tilted or not directly facing the camera.
- The image was compressed too aggressively.
- The printed photo was scaled by the printer instead of printed at actual size.

If your photo is being rejected by an online portal, start by regenerating it from the original image rather than editing the already-compressed file.
