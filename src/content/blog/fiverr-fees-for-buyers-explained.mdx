## FAQ

### Why does the Fiverr buyer total look higher than the gig price?

The buyer total can include service fees and small-order fees. The exact fee shown at checkout is the number to trust.

### Do sellers control Fiverr buyer fees?

No. Sellers can control gig price and package value, but Fiverr controls buyer-side checkout fees.

### Can sellers use buyer-fee math to improve conversion?

Yes. Checking buyer total helps sellers avoid awkward low-price packages where fees feel disproportionate.
