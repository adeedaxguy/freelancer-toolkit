## Step-by-step workflow

1. Calculate seller net from the order value first.
2. List your usual withdrawal method and expected cost.
3. Add currency conversion risk if you do not earn in your home currency.
4. Reserve tax before treating the payment as usable income.
5. Raise package prices if the final bank amount is below your target.

## FAQ

### Does the Fiverr Fee Calculator include withdrawal fees?

It focuses on order-level fees. Add withdrawal, bank, tax, and conversion costs separately because they depend on account, country, and payment method.

### Why do my Fiverr earnings feel lower than the calculator net?

The calculator net can be before taxes and payout friction. Withdrawal costs and conversion can reduce the final received amount.

### Should I add withdrawal costs to my gig price?

Yes, build them into your pricing model if they are recurring and meaningful.
