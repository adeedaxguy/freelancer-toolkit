Printing one passport photo can be more expensive than it should be. A 4x6 print sheet solves that by placing multiple correctly sized photos on one standard photo print.

Use the [Passport Photo 4x6 Print Maker](/tools/passport-photo-4x6-print-maker) to create a printable sheet for 2x2 inch passport-style photos.

## What a 4x6 passport photo sheet does

A normal 4x6 inch photo print is cheap and widely available. Instead of printing one small image, the sheet places multiple ID photos on the same 4x6 canvas.

That is useful when you need spare copies for:

- Passport applications
- Visa applications
- School or office records
- Backup documents
- Family applications

## Important print setting

Print at **actual size**. Do not choose "fit to page" if it changes the dimensions. If the printer scales the sheet, the individual photos may no longer be the right size.

After printing, measure one photo before cutting. A 2x2 inch photo should measure 2 inches wide and 2 inches tall.

## Do not skip official requirements

The print sheet handles layout. It does not guarantee acceptance. Always confirm the current government photo rules for background, expression, glasses, head size, recency, and digital editing.
