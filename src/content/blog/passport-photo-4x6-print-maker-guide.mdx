## Important print setting

Print at **actual size**. Do not choose "fit to page" if it changes the dimensions. If the printer scales the sheet, the individual photos may no longer be the right size.

After printing, measure one photo before cutting. A 2x2 inch photo should measure 2 inches wide and 2 inches tall.

## FAQ

### What is a passport photo 4x6 print sheet?

It is a standard 4x6 photo print that contains multiple small passport-style photos on one sheet so you can print and cut them.

### Can I print passport photos on a normal 4x6 print?

Yes, if the photo size and crop are correct and you print at actual size. Measure one printed photo before using it in an application.

### Should I use the 4x6 print maker before or after cropping?
