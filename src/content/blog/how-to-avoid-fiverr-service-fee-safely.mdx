## FAQ

### Can I avoid Fiverr service fees by moving a buyer off platform?

No. That can violate platform rules and risk your account. Price correctly instead.

### What is the safest way to reduce fee pain?

Build fees into your pricing and create package tiers that make the buyer total feel fair.

### Can buyers avoid Fiverr buyer fees?

The checkout fee is controlled by Fiverr. Buyers can compare packages, but they should rely on the checkout total shown by Fiverr.
