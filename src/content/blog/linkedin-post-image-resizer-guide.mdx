## LinkedIn post image layout tips

Put the most important text or face in the center third of the image. Feed cards can appear at different sizes depending on device, preview type, and placement, so edge-to-edge text is risky even when the exported file has the right dimensions.

Use a wide image for article previews, launch posts, case-study cards, and company updates. If the image is a tall screenshot or a square graphic, use fit mode first to avoid cutting off labels, charts, or UI details.
