## JPG, PNG, or WebP?

Use JPG for photos and scans. Use PNG for graphics, screenshots, and images with sharp text. Use WebP when the original came from a website or modern phone app.

The FreelTools converter accepts common browser-supported image formats and exports a PDF with each image on its own page.
