## 2x2 inch photo basics

For US passport photos, the U.S. Department of State says the photo should be recent, front-facing, taken on a plain white or off-white background, and show a neutral expression with both eyes open. The page also warns that unacceptable photos are a common reason applications are put on hold.

## Best source photo to use

Start with a bright, sharp photo that leaves room around the head and shoulders. The better the source image, the cleaner the 2x2 crop will be.

Good starting photo:

- Face straight toward the camera
- Neutral expression
- Plain white or off-white background
- Even lighting without hard shadows
- High-resolution original photo

Avoid screenshots, heavy beauty filters, or tightly cropped social-media photos. They often look acceptable on your device but fail after resizing or printing.
