## 2x2 inch photo basics

For US passport photos, the U.S. Department of State says the photo should be recent, front-facing, taken on a plain white or off-white background, and show a neutral expression with both eyes open. The page also warns that unacceptable photos are a common reason applications are put on hold.

The resize tool helps with the file size and crop. The official source decides whether the photo is acceptable.
