## Quick answer

Agency project pricing should include every person touching delivery: strategy, design, development, writing, QA, project management, and leadership review. A blended rate prevents one visible role from hiding the real cost.

## Step-by-step workflow

1. List the roles involved in delivery.
2. Estimate hours by role.
3. Convert role hours to a blended internal cost.
4. Add PM, QA, and review time.
5. Apply margin before sending the client price.

## FAQ

### What is a blended agency rate?

It is an average rate across the team roles required to deliver the project.

### Should project management be billed into the price?

Yes. If PM time is required, it belongs in the project cost.

### Can freelancers use this guide?

Yes, especially if they use subcontractors or collaborate with other specialists.
