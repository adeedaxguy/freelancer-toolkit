Use this folder for backlink prospects, broken-link reclamation, guest post ideas, and outreach logs.

## Prospect Quality Rules

Accept prospects with:

- Topical relevance.
- Real editorial standards.
- Real audience or organic visibility.
- Clean outbound link patterns.
- A natural reason to mention a free tool.

Reject prospects with:

- Obvious paid-link footprints.
- Irrelevant niches.
- PBN or link-farm patterns.
- Thin AI-spun posts.
- Casino, adult, pharma, or hacked outbound patterns.
- Exact-match anchor pressure.

Do not send outreach or agree to paid placements without approval.
